# Use Cases

## Indie Hacker

An indie hacker has three project ideas and one weekend. Market Research Engine compares the ideas and shows which one has the clearest audience, strongest pain, and easiest MVP.

## Product Manager

A product manager wants to explore a new feature area. The tool finds competitors, customer complaints, pricing patterns, and signs of market growth.

## Venture Studio

A venture studio screens many raw ideas. The platform produces a consistent first-pass report so the team can decide which ideas deserve interviews.

## Accelerator

An accelerator asks applicants to submit ideas. Market Research Engine creates a short market map for each application and helps mentors focus discussion.

## Developer With A Side Project

A developer wants to know if a technical tool has buyers. The report identifies similar tools, open-source alternatives, paid products, and likely distribution channels.
