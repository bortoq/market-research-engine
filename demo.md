# Demo

## Example Input

```
An app that helps indie hackers test whether a startup idea has a real market.
It should find competitors, demand signals, target users, risks, and the best MVP.
```

## Example Output

### Summary

The idea targets early-stage founders, indie hackers, and product teams who need fast market validation before building. The market is real, but crowded. The strongest angle is not “AI research”, but a repeatable startup decision report with sources, risks, and next experiments.

### Market

- Category: startup research, market intelligence, product validation.
- Buyer: founder, product manager, venture studio, accelerator.
- Willingness to pay: higher for repeat monitoring and deep reports than for one-off summaries.

### Competitors

- Similarweb: strong traffic intelligence.
- SparkToro: audience research.
- Exploding Topics: trend discovery.
- Perplexity/ChatGPT: manual research assistant.

### Differentiator

The user starts from a raw idea and gets a structured business map: market, competitors, pains, target users, risks, and MVP tests.

### Next Experiments

- Interview 10 indie hackers.
- Generate 20 reports for real ideas.
- Ask users which report sections changed their decision.
- Test paid deep report pricing.

## Demo Goal

The first demo should show one complete idea-to-report flow, even if data collection is partially manual.
